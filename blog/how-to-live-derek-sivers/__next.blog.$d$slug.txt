3:Td7c,<p>Some of my favorite quotes from the book:</p>
<h2 id="be-independent">Be independent</h2>
<ul>
<li>All misery comes from dependency.</li>
<li>When you know what you're doing, you won't care what anyone else is doing.</li>
<li>Never agree with anything the same day you hear it, because some ideas are persuasively hypnotic.</li>
<li>Just like you can't read something if it's pushed up against your face, or too far away, you should keep your friends at arm's length — close but not too close.</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="commit">Commit</h2>
<ul>
<li>Stop seeking the best option. Pick one and irreversibly commit.</li>
<li>Word 'decide' comes from Latin <em>'to cut off'</em>.</li>
<li>Enthusiasm is common. Endurance is rare.</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="do-nothing">Do nothing</h2>
<ul>
<li>Desire is the opposite of peace.</li>
<li>Shallow rivers are noisy. Deep lakes are silent.</li>
<li>Most actions are a pursuit of emotions.</li>
<li>Wisdom comes from removing the junk, lies, and obstacles to clear thinking.</li>
<li>The stock market takes money from the active traders and gives it to the patient.</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="think-super-long-term">Think super-long-term</h2>
<ul>
<li>Actions amplify through time to have a massive impact on the future.</li>
<li>Eat mostly vegetables. Exercise always. Get preventative health checkups. Make time for your relationships.</li>
<li>Imagine your future self judging your current life choices.</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="make-memories">Make memories</h2>
<ul>
<li>Monotony is the enemy. Novelty is the solution.</li>
<li>The more memories you create, the longer and richer your life feels.</li>
<li>You do it for the journey, not the destination.</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="master-something">Master something</h2>
<ul>
<li>Mastery is the best goal because the rich can't buy it, the impatient can't rush it, the privileged can't inherit it, and nobody can steal it.</li>
<li>Goals don't improve your future. Goals only improve your present actions.</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="pursue-pain">Pursue pain</h2>
<ul>
<li>If you avoid pain, you avoid improvement. Avoid embarrassment, and you avoid success. Avoid risk, and you avoid reward.</li>
<li><em>'Passion'</em> comes from the Latin word <em>'pati'</em>, meaning <em>'to suffer or endure'</em>.</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="learn">Learn</h2>
<ul>
<li>Confidence is usually ignorance.</li>
<li>Be surprised by something every day.</li>
<li>If you can't explain it yourself, you don't know it.</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="prepare-for-the-worst">Prepare for the worst</h2>
<ul>
<li>A caveman would say, "Oh my god! A glass! What a great invention! I can see what I'm about to drink! This is amazing!"</li>
<li>Fulfillment is more fun than fun.</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="create">Create</h2>
<ul>
<li>You can improve something bad. You can't improve nothing.</li>
<li>Creativity is a magic coin. The more you spend, the more you have.</li>
<li>Originality just means hiding your sources.</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="make-a-million-mistakes">Make a million mistakes</h2>
<ul>
<li>A mistake only counts as experience if you learn from it.</li>
<li>Share your stories from all your mistakes for the benefit of the world.</li>
</ul>
